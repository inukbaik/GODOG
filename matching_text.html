Hi there! I'm interested in your dog training program. Can you tell me more about the pricing?
Absolutely! Our dog training program is $300 for one month of training. This includes access to all the training materials, instructional videos, and personalized support from our team. It's a comprehensive package to help you achieve your training goals.
That sounds like a fair price for the value provided. Can you give me a breakdown of what's included in the program?
Certainly! The program includes weekly training modules that cover various aspects of obedience training and behavior modification. You'll have access to instructional videos demonstrating training techniques and exercises. Additionally, we offer one-on-one consultations with our trainers to address any specific challenges you might face during the training process.
That's great to hear. Is there any flexibility in the program duration? I might need more time to complete the training with my dog.
We understand that each dog progresses at their own pace. While the program is designed to be completed within one month, we do offer some flexibility. If you need more time, we can extend your access to the program materials and provide continued support until you feel confident in your dog's training.
That's excellent. I'm really excited to start the training with my dog. I'm ready to sign up for the program.
You're most welcome! We're thrilled to have you and your dog on board. If you have any further questions or need assistance, don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to support you throughout the training process. Good luck, and enjoy the training experience!
